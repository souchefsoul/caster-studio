<objective>
Eliminate the mobile single-view letterbox gap (caused by `p-4` padding on the content wrapper) and make mobile video playback compatible with iOS autoplay + inline playback policy (via `playsInline` + `muted` HTML attributes). Preserves desktop behavior exactly.

Purpose: On a phone, a single-view image or video currently has ~16px of padding on every side from the `p-4` content wrapper, which wastes viewport width and produces a visible letterbox gap. Also, `<video autoPlay>` without `playsInline` forces iOS Safari to kick into fullscreen on play, and without `muted` iOS may refuse autoplay entirely due to audio-autoplay policy. Both attributes are mandatory for the "plays inline with native controls, no forced fullscreen" contract from CANVAS-05.

Output: `src/components/Canvas.tsx` with the single-view content wrapper padding conditional by breakpoint (`p-0 lg:p-4` when in single view; `p-4` otherwise preserved), and the single-view `<video>` tag carrying `playsInline` + `muted` attributes alongside existing `controls autoPlay loop`.
</objective>
